Reviews is my blog. This is a way for me to express my more random, unconnected thoughts. Since these thoughts are always expressed alongside a book or paper I read recently, I am less likely to be talking completely unsubstantiated bunk. I review whatever I read that I think you might also find interesting, however this must not be constituted as an endorsement of the philosophies or beliefs of the content I am reviewing. Reviews is now a simple blog with not many bells and whistles. It is powered by Jekyll.

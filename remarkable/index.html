FAQs

  1. What are these comics about?
    No idea. Mainly a way to be creative and express my thoughts.
  2. Do you draw these yourself?
    Yep
  3. How do you draw these comics?
    Either on my Remarkable or on the whiteboard. Sometimes on paper. Each comic can take some time to draw.
  4. Where do you get your ideas?
    This is just between you and me, so I hope you won't tell anyone else. Fifteen years ago, I was visited by an apparition in the shape of a bathroom candle. The apparition revealed to me that it was an emissary from a fallen higher power, and would grant me kind-of-funny comic ideas in exchange for my soul. I, of course, agreed. The entity still lives within me, and whispers its hilarious comic ideas to me day after day until eventually I am consigned to the eternal flame of woe.
  5. Can I use your comics in a slideshow/poster/banner?
    Yes, as long as you say they're made by me and don't use them for commercial purposes. If you're unsure whether your use counts as 'commercial', just get in touch.
